About Detectraa

Detectraa is a cutting-edge web application designed for forensic laboratories, research institutions, and educational facilities. Our advanced machine learning algorithms analyze infrared spectra to identify drug compounds with exceptional accuracy.

The platform combines multiple AI models including XGBoost, Random Forest, and ensemble methods to deliver reliable results. Each analysis includes detailed peak matching, confidence scores, and comprehensive PDF reports for documentation and validation purposes.
